## PARTS LIST
For the drone you need:
* Drone Frame
* 4 Brushless motors AA2212
* 4 ESC of 30 Amps
* Flight Controller CC3D
* Arduino PRO Mini or any type of Arduino
* Lithium Polymer Battery 11.1V 
* 1 nrf24l01 

For the RC you need:
* 1 nrf24l01
* LM7805
* LD333V
* Arduino PRO Mini
* Gyroscope
* 2 Joysticks 
* 2 Switches
* Condersators
* Resistors
* 2 Pushbuttons
